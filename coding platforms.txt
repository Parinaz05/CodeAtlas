<div>

</div class="description">
<p>Coding platforms are online environments that allow users to write, compile, execute, and debug code directly from a web browser. They eliminate the need for complex local setups, making them ideal for beginners, students, and developers who want to quickly test or share code. </p>
<p>These platforms are commonly used for learning programming, building small projects, practicing problem-solving, and collaborating with others.</p>
<p>Coding platforms are typically divided into two types: </p>
<ol>
	<li>General-purpose platforms, which support multiple languages</li>
	<li>Language-specific platforms, which are tailored to a particular programming language or development environment</li>
</ol>
<p> Both types offer unique features suited to different coding needs and use cases.</p>
